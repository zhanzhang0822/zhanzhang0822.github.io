    Exhibitions

    Radical Return
    NOV 8 – DEC 12, 2021 | The Boston University Art Gallery (Stone Gallery), Boston, USA
    NOV 20 – DEC 20, 2021 | IS A GALLERY (一弄画廊)， Shanghai, China
    JUL 29 - AUG 19, 2022 | A plus A Gallery, Venice, Italy

    Radical Return is the first group exhibition presented by Radical Characters, a study group and curatorial project that explores the relationship between design and culture in the Chinese and Chinese American community. The selected work features 36 Chinese and Chinese American artists and graphic designers who, using 回 (to return, to turn around, to circle or to reply) as a grid—visually and conceptually, explore acts of return through language, tradition, memory, identity, and history. They work across a range of media to embrace, challenge, and expand what it means to “return” in their artistic practice and in relation to their positions in the world today.

    Sharp Operation: If/Then & Extensive Vision: Umbilical Cord @ Design in pratica.
    SEPT 17 – NOV 1, 2021 | Galleria Cavour, Padova, Italy

    Design in practica. (Design in practice.) is JoeVelluto Studio’s retrospective exhibition curated by Leonardo Caffo. JoeVelluto Studio looks back on twenty years of working activity with examples of design with inherent sense that have the intent to guide the observer’s mindset and daily actions, through a path consisting of eight practices originated from the hybridisation between a pragmatic design-related realness and the Buddhist Noble Eightfold Path, whose fundamentals – narrated in eight video installations conceived in cooperation with Fabrica’s artists in residence – should guide ourselves in our being humans even before being applied to the practice of design.

    The Resting Place @ WHAT IF?
    JUL 27, 2021 – JAN 31, 2022 | Fabrica, Treviso, Italy

    WHAT IF was Fabrica’s 2021 end-of-residency group exhibition featuring 12 works that examine and imagine how the future could be shaped by technology, questioning how the technologies currently integrated into daily life will evolve and which new technologies will emerge.
