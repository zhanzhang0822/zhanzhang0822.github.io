    • Little Talks
    Spring 2014

    Little Talks is a lip-sync music video my friends and I did for Communications Lab video assignment. By emphasizing on the creative transitions between sequences, we aim to take the audience to where NYU Shanghai inaugural class of 2017 spent most of their time within the city，the bund, ECNU, Hexi Cafeteria, Geo building and 九江路 with somehow embarrassing but fun behaviors and interaction in public.

    Filmed and edited by Kevin Pham and Zhang Zhan.
