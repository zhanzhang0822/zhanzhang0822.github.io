    2018 Summer - Ongoing

    I am a music addict. I started collecting albums since 10. I enjoyed the feeling of unboxing albums, viewing the covers and going through lyric booklets. Thus, I decided to design covers for songs or singles that I encounter and fall in love with from time to time as design practice. I also try to picture what I see and express what I feel when listening to these songs via graphics.
