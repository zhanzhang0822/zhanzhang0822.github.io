    • World No Tobacco Day 2021
    Spring 2021

    Cigarette packaging usually features graphic photos and warnings. Although the gruesome images reduce tobacco use, they have become normalized and disregarded. In these two videos I produced with Blender for World No Tobacco Day 2021, I am constructing surreal and contrasting scenarios of regular cigarette packs that contain disturbing imagery used in the packaging instead of tobacco. The visuals as a whole function as a metaphor to highlight the potential severe health risks linked to tobacco.
