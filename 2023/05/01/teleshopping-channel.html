    • Teleshopping Channel
    Spring 2023

    Teleshopping Channel is an envisioned full AI-generated TV channel broadcasting nonstop artificial products with generated scripts, virtual TV hosts and synthesized content. It is a series of video artworks, made with multiple generative AI models and tools, as a social commentary on the current bizarre evolution of consumerism accelerated by the shifting habit of content consumption in the age of mass generated media.

    What would the somewhat aggressive sale tactics of synthesizing empathy and triggering emotions, used by the TV hosts to monetize from the audience watching at home, evolve into with the possible reality of every aspect of living being generated?
